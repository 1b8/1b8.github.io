Kronosville Custom Plugin (OneB) Documentation

Commands
Name Usage Description Aliases Ranks
/dovahzul [E|D] [message] Translate to and from the language of the dragons. [E|D] is the language to translate to, E being English and D being Dovahzul, or Dragon. /dt, /dovah, /dov Donor
/dovahzulchat Toggle Dovahzul chat mode. All messages sent while in Dovahzul chat mode will automatically be translated into Dovahzul. /dc, /dtc, /dchat Op
/pvp Toggle PvP on or off. Players with PvP off cannot damage other players, or be hit by other players themselves. Players with PvP on can hit other players, and can also be damaged by other players. /pvptoggle, /togglepvp All
/addlore [add [lore]|set [lore]|delete] Add lore to the item you're holding. /lore, /al Op
/rename [name] Rename the item you're holding. /name Op
/mailstaff [message] Mail all staff members. /ms All
/oneb reload Reload the config. Op
